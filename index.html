Overview

Get to know our food haven

Know Before You Go New!

At D.S.P BiggioBi Sticklets 9, we're committed to providing you with an authentic culinary experience. Our establishment blends traditional recipes with a modern touch, creating a unique atmosphere for food lovers.

Pair your basundi chai with a warm bun maska or cream roll for the perfect combination of flavors.

Signature Dish

Our signature basundi chai is a creamy, rich tea beverage made with a special blend of spices, milk, and tea leaves. Slow-cooked to perfection, it's the heart of our menu and pairs exquisitely with our freshly baked bun maska or decadent cream rolls.

Our Menu

Delicious offerings for every palate

Basundi Chai

Our signature creamy tea beverage infused with traditional spices and slow-cooked to perfection.

Bun Maska

Soft, warm bun served with generous amounts of creamy butter - the perfect pairing with chai.

Cream Roll

Flaky pastry filled with sweet cream - a delightful dessert to complement your beverage.
